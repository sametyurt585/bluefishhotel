Welcome To Our Hotel

Bluefish Hotel is a stylish and modern hotel located just 7 kilometers from Alanya center. It is 118 kilometers from Antalya Airport and 54 kilometers from Gazipasa Airport, making it easy to reach Alanya. The beach is just 50 meters from the hotel and is easily accessible via an underground passage.

Bluefish Hotel offers a comfortable accommodation experience with 158 rooms. 106 standard rooms, 42 triplex rooms, 8 family rooms and 2 suites, with a total capacity of 380 beds.

Comfortable Holiday Address

The hotel has many services and facilities that you may need during your stay. The rooms have standard equipment such as satellite TV, air conditioning, telephone, safe, hair dryer and balcony.

The services offered to guests in the general areas of the hotel include indoor and outdoor restaurants, lobby bar, pool bar, beach bar, swimming pool, children's pool, children's playground, mini club, shopping stores, doctor services, day and night animations, activities such as table tennis, billiards, beach volleyball, ping-pong, darts, backgammon and chess.Bluefish Hotel offers its guests both a comfortable stay and a fun holiday. While it is in an ideal location for those who want to discover the beauties of Alanya, it also provides you with a pleasant holiday with the facilities within the hotel.
